Solais

Role Programmer & Co-Designer
Engine & Tools Godot, GDScript
Context 1-Month Game Jam (Group Project, 2025)

Solais is a moody, narrative-driven puzzle adventure conceptualized and developed entirely within a one-month Game Jam timeline. The game focuses on atmosphere, environmental storytelling, and methodical puzzle-solving mechanics.

Key Contributions

  • Core Programming: Engineered the foundational gameplay systems using GDScript within the Godot engine.
  • Interactive Systems: Implemented robust environmental interactions and integrated dialogue systems to drive the game's narrative forward.
  • Level & Visual Design: Collaborated closely with the team on level layouts and establishing the game's visual direction to ensure a cohesive and immersive atmosphere.
  • Gameplay Tuning: Continuously iterated on puzzles and movement mechanics throughout the month to balance pacing and player clarity.
