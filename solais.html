Solais

Role Gameplay Designer, Dialogue Designer & Programmer
Engine & Tools Godot, GDScript, Dialogic
Context 2025 GitHub GameOff (1-Month Group Project)

Solais is a narrative-driven game taking its dramatic foundation from the tragic real-life events at the Fair Isle Lighthouse. Step into the unforgiving landscape of WWII Scotland as a Scottish Lighthouse Keeper. Your duty is not just to maintain the light, but to guard your family against German attacks and the encroaching horrors of war.

My Contributions

  • Dialogue Systems & Implementation: Architected the narrative branching system across the 5 in-game days, where player choices directly impact the fate of the family and lead to one of two distinct endings. I utilized the Dialogic plugin to handle complex conversational logic, enabling essential features such as text fast-forwarding and seamless synchronization with the game's voice-acted lines.
  • Gameplay Design: Designed and implemented the core Morse code mechanics to fit the game's historical setting and tension.
  • Systems Programming: Engineered the game's save and load functionality, and collaborated closely with the main gameplay programmer to build out the robust task and quest tracking systems.

Gameplay & Controls

  • Movement & Navigation: Navigate the lighthouse interior using WASD or Arrow Keys. Access the lighthouse Map via the M key and review current objectives using the Q key.
  • Interaction: Seamless environmental interaction handled with the E and R keys.
  • Dialogue: Advance conversation with any input (Spacebar, Enter, or Mouse Click).

Development & Theme Interpretation

Solais was developed in exactly one month as a submission for the 2025 GitHub GameOff by a dedicated 7-person team (alongside voice actors and an independent musician). We pride ourselves on originality: all art, music, voiceovers, writing, and programming were created entirely by our team, with absolutely no premade assets used.

Our interpretation of the jam's theme—"Waves"—went beyond the literal harsh Scottish ocean. Solais (Scottish Gaelic for "light") leans heavily into the concept of light waves as a central design pillar, and even features a specifically wave-oriented minigame.
